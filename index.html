WeKoGP Solution

In the waiting room

Patients' answer simple questions about their health needs and concerns prior to the consultation starting, capturing key health concerns.

Pre-consult

GP receives a clear agenda along with any chronic risks, accessible when they review the patient's files. Allowing them to quickly set a consultation plan ensuring all the patient's expectations are met.

During the consultation

If anything cannot be attended to or chronic disese management is required, a follow-up appointment can effortlessly be set. Best of all, the information seamlessly integrates with major practice management software systems.
